Product Uses

Your physics teachers lectures the class on free body diagrams and gives students a worksheet for homework. You don't remember how to draw a free body diagram, so you scan a QR code on the worksheet with your smartphone.

You can visualize scenarios with overlays of free body diagrams and view them from any angle.

You have a biology test on the skeletal system but have difficulty remembering what the different bones are called when studying off of 2D images.

You open up the Techognize app and view an interactive model of the skeletal system, in which you can see the bones of the human body from any angle. This allows you to retain more of the information.
